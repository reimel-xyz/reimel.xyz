Tasbih Counter Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 July 2026
App: Tasbih Counter (dhikr counter)
Publisher: Reimel Ltd, 159a High Street, Hornchurch, England, RM11 3YD

Tasbih Counter (“the app”) is developed and published by Reimel Ltd. We respect your privacy completely.

Data we collect: none

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. It makes no network connections, and contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Everything you do in the app, your dhikr counts, settings, and history, stays only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

The app uses only the Vibrate permission, to provide tactile feedback when you tap the counter. It does not access your contacts, files, camera, microphone, location, or the internet.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page.

Muslim Budget — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 July 2026
App: Muslim Budget (offline budgeting & zakat calculator)
Publisher: Reimel Ltd, 159a High Street, Hornchurch, England, RM11 3YD

Muslim Budget (“the app”) is developed and published by Reimel Ltd. We respect your privacy completely.

What the app does

Muslim Budget helps you manage your money offline and continuously works out your zakat. You enter your accounts, transactions and assets yourself; the app tracks your zakatable wealth against the nisab and your lunar (hawl) year and tells you when zakat is due. Everything you enter is stored only on your device.

Data we collect: none

The app does not collect, store on our servers, or share any personal or financial data. There is no account, no sign-in, and no cloud, and the app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Your accounts, transactions, balances and settings never leave your device except when you deliberately export or back them up (see below).

Network use

The app works fully offline. It makes only two optional network connections, and neither sends any personal or financial information:

  • Public prices — the app can fetch current gold and silver prices and currency exchange rates so it can value your assets and convert between currencies. This sends only a standard request for public price data — nothing about you, your accounts or your balances is transmitted. You can enter prices manually instead.
  • Google Play Billing — used only if you choose to buy the optional one-time unlock. Payment is handled entirely by Google Play (see below).

Payments

The optional unlock is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Google Play. Reimel Ltd never receives or stores your payment-card details. The app stores only, on your device, whether the unlock is active.

Permissions

  • Internet — used only for the optional public price/exchange-rate fetch and Google Play Billing described above. If you enter prices manually and don’t buy the unlock, no network connection is made.
  • Notifications — to show your zakat-due, nisab, budget and recurring-transaction reminders. Reminders are scheduled and shown entirely on your device.

Backup & export

You can export your data to a JSON file, import one, or set an automatic backup to a folder you choose, using Android’s standard file picker. These files go only where you choose to save them; the app does not upload or transmit them anywhere.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

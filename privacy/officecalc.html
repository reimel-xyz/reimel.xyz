OfficeCalc — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 July 2026
App: OfficeCalc (paper-tape office calculator)
Publisher: Reimel Ltd, 159a High Street, Hornchurch, England, RM11 3YD

OfficeCalc (“the app”) is developed and published by Reimel Ltd. We respect your privacy completely.

Data we collect: none

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. It makes no network connections, and contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Your calculations, paper tapes, sessions, and settings are stored only on your device.

Permissions

The app requests no special permissions. When you choose to share or export a tape (as text or a PDF), that file is handed to the app you pick via Android’s standard share sheet — OfficeCalc does not upload or transmit it anywhere.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page.

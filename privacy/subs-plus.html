Subs+ Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 July 2026
App: Subs+ (subscription tracker & renewal reminder)
Publisher: Reimel Ltd, 159a High Street, Hornchurch, England, RM11 3YD

Subs+ (“the app”) is developed and published by Reimel Ltd. We respect your privacy completely.

What the app does

Subs+ helps you keep track of the subscriptions you pay for. You enter each subscription yourself; the app shows your total monthly and yearly spend and reminds you in time to cancel before a renewal or notice period closes. Everything you enter is stored only on your device.

Data we collect: none

The app does not collect, store on our servers, or share any personal data. There is no account, no sign-in, and no cloud, and the app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Your subscriptions, prices, notes and settings never leave your device except when you deliberately export them (see below).

Network use

The app’s only network connection is an optional website-icon fetch: when you add a subscription and provide its website, the app can download that site’s icon (favicon / logo) purely to display it next to the subscription. This sends only a standard web request for the icon, no personal data, no list of your subscriptions, and nothing identifying you is transmitted. This feature can be turned off in the app’s settings, after which the app makes no network connections at all.

Permissions

  • Internet, used only for the optional website-icon fetch described above. If you disable icon fetching, no network connection is made.
  • Notifications, to show your renewal / cancel-by reminders on the day you asked for. Reminders are scheduled and shown entirely on your device.

Backup & export

You can export your data to a JSON file, or import one, using Android’s standard file picker. These files go only where you choose to save them; the app does not upload or transmit them anywhere.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

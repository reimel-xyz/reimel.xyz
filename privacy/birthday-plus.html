Birthday+ Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 July 2026
App: Birthday+ (birthday & occasion reminder)
Publisher: Reimel Ltd, 159a High Street, Hornchurch, England, RM11 3YD

Birthday+ (“the app”) is developed and published by Reimel Ltd. We respect your privacy completely.

What the app does

Birthday+ stores the birthdays and special occasions you care about and reminds you in time. Everything you add is stored only on your device.

Data we collect or transmit: none

The app makes no network connections, it has no internet permission at all. There is no account and no cloud, and the app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Nothing the app reads or creates ever leaves your device.

Contacts

If you choose to import birthdays from your contacts, the app reads your device’s contacts only at that moment and only to show you a list of people and dates you can pick from. This happens entirely on your device. Contact data is never uploaded, never shared, and never sent anywhere. If you don’t use contact import, the app never reads your contacts. You can also add birthdays manually or import them from a file instead.

Permissions

  • Read contacts, optional, used only when you tap to import birthdays from your contacts, as described above. You can decline it and still use the app fully.
  • Notifications, to show your birthday and occasion reminders. Reminders are scheduled and shown entirely on your device.

Importing & backup

You can import birthdays from a file (for example a CSV, vCard, calendar or another app’s backup) and export your own JSON backup, using Android’s standard file picker. These files are read from, and saved to, only the locations you choose; the app does not upload or transmit them anywhere.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page.

Eng-Alb Dictionary — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 July 2026
App: Eng-Alb Dictionary (offline English–Albanian dictionary)
Publisher: Reimel Ltd, 159a High Street, Hornchurch, England, RM11 3YD

Eng-Alb Dictionary (“the app”) is developed and published by Reimel Ltd. We respect your privacy completely.

Data we collect: none

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The dictionary works fully offline from a database bundled inside the app. It contains no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Your searches and search history are stored only on your device and can be cleared at any time.

Permissions

The app requests no special permissions for its functionality.

Links to other apps

If you tap “Search on Wiktionary” for a word, the app opens that word’s page in your device’s web browser. From that point you are on the Wiktionary website, governed by its own privacy practices — the dictionary app itself sends nothing.

Children

The app is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page.
